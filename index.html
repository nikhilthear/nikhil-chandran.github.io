Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How is your service different from automated email campaigns?
We manually send and manage every email and follow-up, ensuring each message feels personal and natural. This increases open and reply rates, helps avoid spam filters, and builds trust. Automation lacks this touch, leading to lower engagement.
Q2: What kind of results can I expect from your service?
• Email open rates of 30%–50% (vs. 15%–25% industry average)
• Reply rates between 6%–12%
• Meeting bookings of 2%–5% of contacts reached
• A 20%–40% increase in qualified booth traffic
• Faster post-show lead conversion through timely follow-ups
Q3: How soon before the trade show should I start the campaign?
We recommend starting your pre-show outreach 2–3 months in advance. This builds awareness, schedules meetings, and generates buzz.
Q4: What kind of data do you use for the email campaigns?
We provide a clean, verified, bounce-free contact database tailored to your industry and target audience. Post-campaign, the full list is yours for reuse.
Q5: Do you handle post-show follow-ups?
Yes! We offer manual email campaigns with up to 3 follow-ups per lead to convert booth visitors into customers.
Q6: What if I don’t have much time or staff to manage marketing?
No worries — we handle all outreach logistics. Your team focuses on the show while we manage email campaigns, lead tracking, and reports.
Q7: Can you help me book meetings before the show?
Absolutely. We can integrate booking tools like Calendly to make it easy for prospects to schedule demos or visits in advance.
Q8: How do you measure campaign success?
We provide weekly reports on emails sent, open rates, replies, booked meetings, and engagement — full transparency throughout.
Q9: What industries or company sizes do you work best with?
We work well across most B2B industries — from tech to healthcare. Targeting is customized for your customer profile, whether you're a startup or enterprise.
Q10: What if I’m new to trade shows — is this service still for me?
Definitely! Our campaigns help newcomers build awareness quickly and maximize ROI. We guide you every step of the way.
